V8 INSTITUTIONAL KEY LEVEL DETECTOR - COMPLETE LOGIC
====================================================

STEP 1: MAJOR SWING DETECTION (5-BAR PIVOTS)
- Scan monthly candles for swing highs: high[i] > high[i±5] for ALL 5 bars left/right
- Scan for swing lows: low[i] < low[i±5] for ALL 5 bars left/right
- Only major extremes that capped multi-month trends qualify

STEP 2: ZONE CLUSTERING
- Group swing highs within 0.4-1.2x monthly ATR(14)
- Group swing lows within same tolerance
- Require MINIMUM 3 swing points to form a valid zone
- Calculate zone mid, top, bottom

STEP 3: REACTION VALIDATION (BRUTAL FILTER)
For each potential zone:
  a) Count historical touches (price enters zone)
  b) For each touch, verify REAL REVERSAL:
     - Must produce 500-1500+ pip move away (5-15% for EURUSD scale)
     - Must occur within 3-6 months after touch
     - Wick rejection preferred (strong monthly wick at zone)
  c) REJECT if < 3-4 confirmed reactions
  d) REJECT if no significant reversals (all weak bounces)

STEP 4: CONFLUENCE SCORING
- Round number proximity: +2 points
- All-time/decade high-low: +3 points
- Major Fib level (50%, 61.8% of multi-year swing): +2 points
- Historical significance (15+ year old level): +1 point

STEP 5: RECENT BREAK CHECK
- If price closed >1.5x ATR beyond zone in last 12-24 months WITHOUT retest
- REJECT or heavily downgrade (broken level)

STEP 6: STRENGTH SCORING (1-10)
- Touches: (touches / 7) * 4.0  (max 4 points for 7+ touches)
- Reversal Quality: (big_reversals / touches) * 3.0  (max 3 points for 100% quality)
- Confluence: confluence_score * 0.2  (max 2 points)
- Recency: 1.0 if tested in last 24 months, 0.5 if 24-60 months, 0 if older

STEP 7: OUTPUT TOP 4-7 ONLY
- Sort by score descending
- Output only scores >= 6.0 (institutional grade)
- Maximum 7 levels per pair (prevent clutter)

VISUALIZATION:
- Thick horizontal zones (thicker = higher score)
- Approach lines (dotted)
- Touch circles with dates
- Reversal arrows with pip magnitude
